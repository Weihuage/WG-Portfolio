About me

I'm a UX designer and technical builder at Amazon, working on satellite connectivity experiences for the Leo (Kuiper) program. I design console interfaces that help maritime and mobility customers manage complex networks with clarity and confidence.

My approach blends design thinking with hands-on prototyping. I believe the best experiences come from understanding both the customer's mental model and the technical constraints of the system. I work closely with engineers, PMs, and stakeholders to ship solutions that are simple on the surface and powerful underneath.

Before Amazon, I worked on enterprise SaaS products and internal tools, always focused on making complex workflows feel approachable. I'm passionate about design systems, information architecture, and the space where business strategy meets pixel-level craft.

When I'm not designing, I'm learning to code — building prototypes in React and exploring how AI can accelerate the design-to-development handoff.
