Fleet Management Redesign

Research & Design · 2026 · Amazon Kuiper

Overview

Fleet management allows customers to organize their vessels and vehicles into logical groups, assign service plans, and monitor connectivity at scale. The original design was a flat list that didn't scale beyond 20 assets.

Challenge

Enterprise customers with 100+ assets struggled to find specific vessels, couldn't bulk-assign configurations, and had no way to see fleet-wide health at a glance. Support tickets related to fleet management increased 3x quarter over quarter.

Approach

  • Analyzed support tickets to identify top pain points
  • Ran card-sorting sessions to understand customers' mental models for grouping
  • Designed a hierarchical fleet structure with folders and tags
  • Added bulk actions, inline status indicators, and a fleet health summary
  • Validated with 5 enterprise customers through moderated usability testing

Outcome

Task completion time for "assign a service plan to a group" dropped from 8 clicks to 3. Fleet-related support tickets decreased by 45% in the first month post-launch.

Key Decisions

  • Folder-based hierarchy over flat tags: matched how customers already think about their fleets
  • Inline editing: no modal dialogs for common actions like rename or move
  • Persistent filter bar: customers keep returning to the same subset of assets
